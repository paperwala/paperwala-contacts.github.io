About Us

Paper Wala is a business that focuses on recycling paper,plastic and metalic items giving back to the community. The business accepts all kinds of paper,metalic and plastic materials, including newspapers, magazines,tins ,rods ,buckets ,bottles and cardboard, and they recycle the papers by giving it to factories that will recycle the paper,metal and plastic and make new products out of it. What makes Paper Wala stand out is its unique program that gives the customer 50% of the money we earn from the paper,metals and plastic they gave for recycling. For every kg of paper,metal and plastic that a customer recycles, they earn a certain amount of money that can be redeemed for cash or used towards future purchases. This not only encourages customers to recycle more but also helps the business to reduce waste and promote sustainability. In addition to providing a valuable service to the community,Paper Wala is fully eco-friendly. Paper Wala is commited to make tommorow a better and greener day.
